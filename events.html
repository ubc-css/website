Events

Events


The CSS offers a variety of exciting events for members, including (but not limited to) academic events to guide you through your COGS degree, workshops with amazing industry partners, and social events to connect with some great COGS-y people!

Additionally, the COGS lounge is there for all COGS students (open 9:00AM - 5:00PM on weekdays), so feel free to drop by if you ever need a place to study or relax!
