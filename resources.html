Need 402 help?

Stay tuned via Instagram and Discord for our bi-annual Gearing Up for 402 events, where you'll hear from COGS students and alumni with first-hand experience.

Check out the UBC COGS 402 page.

Please also have a look at the 402 Collaboration Agreement and Supervisor Evaluation Form. Know what's expected of you going into your 402!

In the end, the best way to learn more about COGS 402 is to talk with COGS students who've completed their 402. Each 402 project is unique to the person doing it. There is no one size fits all!
